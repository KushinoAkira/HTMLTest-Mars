Mars is a dusty, cold, desert world with a very thin atmosphere. There is strong evidence Mars was – billions of years ago – wetter and warmer, with a thicker atmosphere.

ABOUT THE MARS

•Equatorial radius: 3396.2 ± 0.1 km

•Polar radius: 3376.2 ± 0.1 km

•Mass: 6.4171×1023 kg

•Surface gravity: 3.72076 m/s2

3 Need-to-Know Things About Mars

  1. SMALL PLANET

    2. If the Sun were as tall as a typical front door, Earth would be the size of a dime, and Mars would be about as big as an aspirin tablet.

  2. FOURTH ROCK

    3. Mars orbits our Sun, a star. Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun at an average distance of about 228 million km (142 million miles) or 1.52 AU.

  3. LONGER DAYS

    4. One day on Mars takes a little over 24 hours. Mars makes a complete orbit around the Sun (a year in Martian time) in 687 Earth days.
